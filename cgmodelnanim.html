Cinnamon Cutout Ornaments

I made these as a teenager to have something to sell alongside my mother's basket garters and other goods. I have been trying to get back into making these for personal use and local ordering. I was without all of my ingredients at one point, but I had the cookie cutters for the shapes. Per suggestion, I traced the cutters into an SVG, a vector file. Inside Blender I was able to make an extrusion. The material is a noisy diffuse with a texturized alpha.

Scenes and Environment

Tea Time

This tea set is based on one of my high school arts and crafts assignments. The inspiration comes from a movie that took place in WWII era Japan. The tea set is pink with aquamarine writing. The pot has "o-tya," which means tea or green tea. The cups, purposely made without handles, had names for two friends. Unfortuneately, the set was not glaze fired in time to be used. I decided that I could at least have the set digitally. The environment, my dining area at the time, was rendered in the new Cycles engine.

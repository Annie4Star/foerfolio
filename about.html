The designer, Ann-Marie Foerster

I am a graduate of The Ohio State University with a BFA in Art & Technology. The program caters to new media art, featuring basic robotics, motion and video, 3D, and image editing. My personal concentration was in video editing, which complimented my minor in Film Studies.

I started teaching myself web design in high school, making basic web pages for illustration projects. I still have the book that got me started, even though it is extremely outdated by this point.

My parents, notably my father, fostered a love for the arts in me and my brothers. We were raised on 'Singing in the Rain,' over-annunciating Johann Sebastian Bach, and always having my father's few framed prints of Ansel Adams and Andrew Wyeth in view.

I am currently located in Columbus, where I participate in cartoonists' galleries and geting my name out there. I frequent Reddit and Stack Exchange seeking design advice. I am working on a Front End certificate with Free Code Camp, and trying to get javascript to sink in.

Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is written to be as comprehensive and transparent as possible. Opted in users will be notified any time the policy changes.

What Sort of Information is Collected?

The contact form on this website collects first and last names, as well as email addresses. If provided the website also potentially collects physical addresses and phone numbers. The provided information could be of individuals or businesses.

Paid jobs will require the provision of payment information through physical or digital invoices. This could be banking or Paypal information.

How is Your Information Used?

Your information will be used to reply to service inquiries and provide them. Contact information is required for the provision of services, mockups, and final designs or products. It will also be used for the purchase of said goods and services, and any resulting invoices.

The only third parties that may come into contact with website data include Google, email client Gmail, and potentially Paypal.

How is Your Information Protected?

This website uses an SSL certificate to keep the website secure. Third parties are vetted for security and business practices. Steps are taken to prevent breaches or attacks on personal information.

The message respondent(s) and site administrator(s) carefully inspect all incoming emails, links, and attachments. Suspicious links are never followed, and shortlinks are checked in a trusted reversal location. Generally, websites are visited directly instead of followed on email links.

In Case of a Breach

If there is a breach all stored data will be purged after a notice is sent. Notices will include a link to be put back on any mailing lists.

GDPR Information

The website works to be GDPR compliant. The privacy policy is written in normal terms and not legal terms. Users and visitors understand that by providing data they are consenting to the website using it to provide its services.

Any requests to remove personal data from the website will be honored. This is only for data not involved in an active project or invoice.

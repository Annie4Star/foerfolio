A professional web and graphic designer with eight years of experience.

Client Responsibilities

The client will:

  • Have a project proposal to present to the designer, ideally with some color swatches or fonts in mind.
  • Provide company assets such as logos, images, fonts (if applicable under license), color codes, ect. to the designer.
  • Only expect material from the designer that is within her ability to do (design, FTP, WordPress setup, ect.).
  • Have a respectable due date for the type and scope of a project (see Table A below).
  • Consider all designer suggestions for changes in font, layout, color, ect.
  • Pay all invoices in a prompt manner. Plans for payment based on future profits are not accepted.
  • Understand that the designer may turn down or cancel any project or portion of project at any time, for any reason.
  • Respect that the designer may have other projects ahead in queue, and business hours that the designer may not work or read messages outside of.

Designer Responsibilities

The designer will:

  • Be prompt in answering all past, present, and future client questions (within three business days).
  • Update the client on project progress per contract details, once or twice weekly, or as questions arise.
  • Create all work material according to client specifications, with or without approved suggestions.
  • Complete the work by the contract specified due date.
  • If the designer cannot complete the work by the due date, or an emergency comes up, the designer will contact the client immediately.

There may be a scenario where the designer may see a delay in completion, or may not be able to complete the project by the due date. Incomplete stages of work may be refunded (if paid) and all (complete or in progress) material transferred. This is only in the case of a designer initiated cancellation.

Accepted Forms of Payment and Process

The designer accept payments through check or Paypal business invoices. The designer, Ann-Marie Foerster, does not accept payments in gift cards, phone cards, service exchange, memberships or subscriptions, material goods, or consumable goods.

The designer does not work for free or for exposure.

The client will not receive the finished and unmarked work until they have paid in full. Late fees may apply.

Standard Fees

Standard fees or expenses included in all jobs are:

  • Nonrefundable deposit and consultation fee - 20% of the estimate (only required if the estimate exceeds $500)
  • Do not display or exclusivity fee - $50
  • Rights to for profit duplication or monetization - $500
  • Modification rights - $50
  • Rush job fee - Estimate + 25%
  • Kill or cancellation fee - Completed portion + 25%
  • Handover of material in client canceled project - Completed portion of project + 5%
  • Late payment fees - Compounded from invoice amount and wait time (see Table B below)
  • Material purchases fee - Dependent on costs to designer
    • Assets, materials, or software exclusive to the project - Full Cost to designer
    • Assets, materials, and software the designer may use for other projects - 20% of cost to designer
  • Printing and shipping - Dependent on costs to designer

Delivery of Completed Work

The designer, Ann-Marie Foerster, will deliver the full size completed work in digital and/or physical format upon final approval and full payment. This may also include an email attachment, envelope, package, or flash drive, of the digital or physical material created and used. This does not include licensed or non-transferrable material such as programs and fonts.

Client Rights to Work

Use

The client is granted the exclusive right to use finished work and reproductions for display, advertising, and social media. The client may reproduce the work for monetized means, such as merchandising, if duplication rights are paid.

Storage on Designer End

The designer, Ann-Marie Foerster, will save all files on a private drive. The duration of storage, unless otherwise specified in the contract, is three (3) years from the delivery date upon final approval.

All clients will be notified six (6) and three (3) months before deletion of files. Clients will also be notified in the case of a drive failure.

Designer Rights to Work

Display and Use

The designer, Ann-Marie Foerster, retains the rights to display finished and unfinished work in print and online. Online presence includes this website (annmariefoerster.com), LinkedIn, Behance, Dribble, and social media platforms such as Twitter and Instagram. This is for advertising purposes of services to other potential clients.

The designer, Ann-Marie Foerster, understands that clients may desire confidentiality of their projects until a given date. Clients may also wish to be kept anonymous during the duration of the project, or even after the project is complete. This stipulation may be included in a drafted contract. The designer, Ann-Marie Foerster, will honor this reveal request. Failure to do so will be understood as breach of contract. There is no fee attached to this request.

Clients have the right to exclusivity of their projects and completed work. The designer, Ann-Marie Foerster, will continue to keep a copy of all files for the standard three (3) years. However, the designer, Ann-Marie Foerster, will not display the work in their portfolio. Doing so will be considered a breach of contract on the behalf of the designer, Ann-Marie Foerster.

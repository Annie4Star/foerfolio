I have experience working in the Adobe programs AfterEffects and Premiere. In addition to these programs I have a background in the Apple video programs iMovie, Final Cut Pro, and Apple Motion. Sound is usually edited and mixed in Audacity, and placed over the ducked original audio. Common edits include de-essing (removing vocal peaks), removing background noises, volume correction, and equate and reverb.
